Super Eco – Pioneering the Future of Electric Mobility

Innovation
About Us

Super Eco is a leading name in the electric vehicle (EV) industry, committed to transforming urban mobility with innovative, efficient, and eco-friendly transportation solutions. Our journey began in 2012, driven by a vision to create a cleaner and more sustainable future. October 2013 we formally registered as SuperEco Automotive Co. LLP and commenced commercial production, establishing ourselves as pioneers in India's growing EV sector.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision for the Future

Our Vision

At Super Eco, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of electric mobility. Our mission is to:

Our Mission

  1. Revolutionize urban transportation with smart, sustainable, and high-performance electric vehicles.
  2. Invest in cutting-edge battery technology for longer range and faster charging solutions.
  3. Expand our presence in both domestic and international markets, bringing Made-in-India electric vehicles to the world.

As the EV industry evolves, Super Eco continues to lead with innovation, quality, and a strong commitment to sustainability.

Our Journey & Milestones

2012

The Vision Begins

Super Eco’s story began with a deep commitment to sustainable transportation. Recognizing the future potential of electric vehicles, our founders began working on the concept, researching global trends and technologies.

2013

Official Incorporation & Production Launch

Super Eco was officially registered as SuperEco Automotive Co. LLP in October 2013. We launched our commercial production, setting new standards in electric mobility.

2014

Leading the Three-Wheeler EV Revolution

Our journey began with the production of electric three-wheelers, including:

  • TumTum – A passenger electric auto designed for urban and last-mile connectivity.
  • E-Cargo (Vazra) – A powerful electric cargo vehicle for efficient and eco-friendly logistics.

Super Eco was among the first companies in India to receive ICAT certification for its electric three-wheelers, solidifying its credibility and market presence.

2015

Revolutionizing Two-Wheelers with Lithium Technology

With the introduction of lithium battery technology in the two-wheeler industry, we identified a significant opportunity to innovate. While many manufacturers were still reliant on lead-acid batteries, we took a bold step forward and became:

The first company in India to start commercial production of electric scooters solely powered by lithium batteries.

This move set Super Eco apart as an industry leader, focusing on:

  • Higher efficiency & longer battery life
  • Faster charging & superior performance
  • Sustainable & cost-effective transportation
Founders

Our Founders

Mr. Ramji Das Agrawal (Chairman)

A veteran entrepreneur with over five decades of business experience, Mr. Ramji Das Agrawal has successfully managed ventures across iron rolling mills, tin manufacturing, international trade, and industrial supplies. His expertise in manufacturing and global trade has played a crucial role in shaping Super Eco into a trusted name in the electric mobility space.

He holds an M.Com degree with an LLB in Business Law, giving him deep insights into business operations, finance, and legal frameworks. His leadership and strategic vision have been instrumental in guiding Super Eco’s journey toward innovation and excellence.

Mr. Shailesh Agrawal (Managing Director)

With a strong background in marketing, finance, and international trade, Mr. Shailesh Agrawal brings a global perspective to Super Eco. He holds an MBA in Marketing and Finance and began his career managing the family’s import business in Mumbai.

Between 2007 and 2013, he expanded his expertise by working in Singapore and Malaysia, actively engaging in overseas trading. During this period, he closely studied the electric vehicle industry, collaborated with leading manufacturers, engineers, and technocrats, and gained hands-on experience in EV technology and production processes. Equipped with this knowledge, he returned to India to establish Super Eco as a leading EV brand
